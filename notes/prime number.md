# prime number

Belphegor's prime 
`1 000 000 000 000 066 600 000 000 000 001` `= 10³⁰ + 666 + 10¹⁴ + 1`
- palindromic prime number with 666 in the middle and 13 0s on either side, named after seven princes of hell
